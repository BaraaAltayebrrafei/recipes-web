Who We Are ?

We are three girls students in the first year of the university majoring in informatic.
We decided to make a website with food content to help people and spread some foods to the whole world.
We were not satisfied with this only, but we also worked to make this project a success by programming it as well as we could and providing it with the largest amount of recipes.
We have provided this website with many delicious recipes from around the world, and we have also added the purchase feature by requesting the recipe from its publisher. We also added to this website the social media character through the comment box and the like button so that users of this website can communicate and many other features.
We hope that you like this website, and we are waiting for you to send messages via e-mail to tell us about any ideas to develop this website better, or to show us how much you like it.
